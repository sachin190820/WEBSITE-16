Slowdown

Services

Product photogragrafy,Art direction,Editorial design

Description

Slowdown Studio is an art-inspired home goods brand that collaborates with various artists from around the world to create unique and visually appealing designs that are then applied to their products.

As most of Slowdown Studio’s leads come from social media marketing, having visually strong product pictures is crucial for their sales. Slowdown Studio partnered with us to capture the striking beauty of their blankets. From catalog to ads to website visuals, our creative photography complements the vibrancy of their designs and has helped them increase online sales.
